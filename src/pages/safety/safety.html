Safety

  • Please ensure that you are wearing comfortable and appropriate clothing and footwear before carrying out a test.
  • If at any point you feel like you need a break then please stop and rest. The timer will continue to count down unless you explicitly stop the test, allowing you to continue at any time.
  • If you feel any shortness of breath, dizziness, or any other symptoms out of the ordinary, or you feel like you can't continue the test for any other reason then please stop the test immediately and seek medical advice if necessary.
  • It is at the user's discretion where they carry out a test, but ideally the test should be carried out on flat ground. please be aware when crossing roads, walkways, and paths. Ideally you should carry out a test in a clear, traffic free area as per the instructions below.

Instructions

  • On pressing the 'Start Test' button the test will start immediately.
  • If you have never carried out a test before then the app will first show you this safety and instructions dialogue. On closing the dialogue, simply click 'Start Test' again to begin.
  • The app will calculate your distance by tracking your GPS location so please carry out the test in a clear, open and traffic free environment with your location services turned on, high accuracy enabled, and please ensure the app has location permissions.
  • You should walk for the duration of the 6 minutes, taking any breaks as required. A result will only be recorded if you complete the full 6 minutes without stopping the test.
  • The test can be stopped at any time by clicking the 'Stop Test' button, followed by the 'Confirm' button in the corresponding popup.
  • The timer will continue to countdown until you click the 'Confirm' button on the aforementioned popup.
  • On completion of a full 6 minutes, you will be shown your results along side some comparisons of your previous results and how your latest result compares with people of a similar age.
  • You can view all your previous results at any time in the 'Log' section of the app.
